Data Description for "Multi-centennial variability of the Afro-Asian monsoon driven by Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation"

All data files have the first column representing time and the second column representing the data.

RAP_v1_1470-2013.nc
A gridded 544-year (AD 1470–2013) summer rainfall reconstruction of the Asian Summer Monsoon (ASM) land region. 

shi2014sasmi-noaa.csv
A South Asian Summer Monsoon Index (SASMI) reconstructed over the past millennium (AD 1000–2013) using 15 tree-ring chronologies.

IGGPRE.1.0.anom.nc
A May–September precipitation reconstruction for China spanning AD 1470–2000. 

Songya-sue-rsp3-linear-498.6-LOWESS.txt
A stalagmite δ¹⁸O record from Songya Cave.

HotiCave-sue-rsp2-linear-787.2-LOWESS-past1000.txt
A stalagmite δ¹⁸O record from Hoti Cave.

LakeBosumtwi.csv
A proxy dataset from Lake Bosumtwi, Ghana, representing the North African Summer Monsoon (NAFSM) over the past millennium.
